Cost-effective, AI-based Last Mile Targeting Solution for Drones

Our Product

We develop AI-based software for the recognition and autonomous engagement of air and ground targets. Our solution can be integrated with fixed-wing kamikaze drones and multi-rotors running Ardupilot.

AI Target Recognition

Our neural network, trained on a proprietary dataset, capably recognizes various target classes including UAVs, vehicles, and personnel.

GPS-Independent Autonomy

After target acquisition, the onboard computer autonomously guides the UAV. This process is independent of GPS, making it resilient to spoofing and jamming.

Advanced Simulation

We utilize Gazebo, AirSim, and SITL simulations, alongside custom solutions and integration testing, to rapidly validate hypotheses and enhance field trial effectiveness.

Thermal Camera Integration

Our system is fully capable of identifying and tracking targets using thermal imaging, ensuring operational effectiveness in various visibility conditions, day or night.
